# Quick Start Guide - Financial Markets

Welcome to Course 1! Here's how to get the most out of this course.

## Course Overview

**Duration**: 7 Weeks  
**Focus**: Introduction to professional finance - markets, products, participants, and regulation

## What You'll Learn

1. **Financial Markets Fundamentals**
   - Markets, products, participants, regulation
   - How financial systems work

2. **Financial Mathematics**
   - Present and forward values
   - Cash flow discounting
   - Return and volatility calculations

3. **Risk & Modeling**
   - Credit risk factors
   - Financial models
   - Risk measurement

4. **Market Activities**
   - Trading, financing, brokering
   - Pricing, hedging, optimizing
   - Risk management
